Help on help...

Errors those may arrive when you click on a topic in the Help/Documentation menu of Avro Keyboard.

1.

Cause : Adobe Acrobat Reader is not installed on your system.

Solution: Consider downloading free Acrobat Reader from here.

2.

Problem : I've Acrobat Reader installed on my computer. But I cannot read Avro Keyboard help files!

Cause : For reading Avro Keyboard help files, you need at least version 5 of Adobe Acrobat Reader. With earlier versions, you cannot read Bangla text in pdf.

Solution: Consider downloading latest version of Adobe Acrobat Reader from here.

(Note: Appearance of the above mentioned error messages may differ in different windows versions.)
